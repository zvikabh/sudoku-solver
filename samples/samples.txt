This directory contains three sample puzzles for the Sudoku Solver program.

To load a puzzle, double-click on it, and then click Yes. Then, run the 
Sudoku Solver and the puzzle will be loaded.

The Sudoku Solver is an open-source Windows application for solving Sudoku
(Number Place) puzzles. You can download it for free from
http://www.technion.ac.il/~zvikabh/software .
